Team

Check our Team

Milind Pant

Chief Executive Officer


Milind Pant joined Amway in January 2019 as Chief Executive Officer. He leads the overall management of Amway and is responsible for setting the company’s strategic vision and growth strategies. Pant reports directly to and holds a seat on Amway’s Board of Directors. He is the first non-family member to run the business since its founding in 1959.In his role as CEO, Pant serves on Amway’s Global Leadership Team, Amway’s key executive decision-making body focused on global strategy development and delivering meaningful ABO and customer experiences.





Asha Gupta

CHIEF STRATEGY & CORPORATE
DEVELOPMENT OFFICER


Asha Gupta is the Chief Strategy & Corporate Development Officer. In her role, she is responsible for leading the development of Amway’s long-term global business strategy and evaluating strategic growth opportunities to help Amway Business Owners (ABOs) meet customer needs and grow their business online. In addition to her enterprise strategy stewardship, she will also manage strategic acquisitions and alliance initiatives for the company.Asha reports directly to CEO Milind Pant. She serves on Amway’s Global Leadership Team, Amway’s key decision-making body focused on strategy development and delivering meaningful ABO and customer experiences.





Anouchah Sanei

CHIEF INNOVATION & SCIENCE OFFICER


Anouchah Sanei is Amway’s Chief Innovation and Science Officer and oversees Amway Innovation and all global research and development for Amway, which includes nearly 1,000 scientists, engineers and technicians throughout more than 100 countries and territories where Amway operates.Sanei reports directly to Amway CEO Milind Pant and serves on Amway’s Global Leadership Team, Amway’s key executive decision making body focused on global strategy development and delivering meaningful ABO and customer experiences.





John Parker

CHIEF SALES OFFICER & REGIONAL
PRESIDENT WEST


As Chief Sales Officer & Regional President West, John Parker is responsible for Amway’s global sales strategy and operations, and oversees distributor incentives and recognition, field leadership development, and global training and education. He is also responsible for Amway’s Russia, Europe, Southern Africa, Australia and New Zealand markets as well as its North America and Latin America regions.Parker reports directly to Amway CEO Milind Pant. He serves on Amway’s Global Leadership Team, Amway’s key executive decision-making body focused on global strategy development and delivering meaningful ABO and customer experiences.
